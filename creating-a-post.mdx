## The Content Studio

The Content Studio (`/generate`) is where most users spend their time. Every session starts the same way: pick a theme with **Genie**, then generate.

## Picking a Theme with Genie

Before generating, you choose a content theme. **Genie** is an AI suggestion engine that reads your business profile — industry, services, location, and real Google review keywords — and offers four specific post angles tailored to your business.

Examples for a massage therapist:
- "Deep tissue for desk workers — the Monday morning fix"
- "Customer spotlight: Sarah's 12-session transformation"
- "Weekend self-care: booking window closes Friday at noon"
- "What clients say about our hot stone add-on"

You can accept any Genie suggestion or type your own theme. Genie also factors in:

| Signal | What it does |
|---|---|
| **Content balance** | Tracks whether you've been over-posting promotions vs. education vs. personal content, and suggests the missing type |
| **Weather signals** | Adds weather-driven angles for relevant industries when rain, heat, or storms are forecast |
| **Day of week** | Surfaces weekend-urgency themes for retail and food businesses on Fridays |
| **Review themes** | Always includes one angle referencing real Google review keywords from your customers |

## Passenger Mode

The default experience for all users. You click **Generate** and get:

<Steps>
  <Step title="A batch of polished text ideas" icon="pen-line">
    Each idea includes a post concept, caption, call to action, and timing recommendation — everything ready to use.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tinder-style swipe review" icon="hand">
    Swipe right to save an idea, left to skip it, or tap to edit the caption inline before saving.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Quick Visual for saved ideas" icon="image">
    For each saved idea, request a **Quick Visual** — a single photorealistic image (2 credits). The complete idea card is then ready to drop into a Week Pack.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Passenger Mode is intentionally frictionless. If you just want good content fast without thinking about image settings, stay here.
</Callout>

## Pilot Mode

Pilot Mode unlocks for Pro users after 7 days and activates the **Antigravity Drawer** — a full image-planning interface.

<Callout kind="info">
  The 7-day unlock is a drip gate designed to ensure you've developed a content rhythm before adding image complexity. It's granted automatically by a nightly job — no action needed on your part.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Pilot Mode drawer and set your preferences" icon="sliders">
    Configure before generating:
    - **Show draft options first** vs. go straight to final
    - **Include text in image** (uses the best text-in-image AI model)
    - **Identity strictness** — whether to use your profile face photos
    - **Style override** — optional prompt addition like "vintage 35mm film" or "clean product photography"
  </Step>

  <Step title="Orchestrate Plan" icon="brain">
    PostGlider calls an AI planning agent that reads the post context, your profile, available vault photos, and your drawer controls, then outputs a deterministic plan: quality tier, prompt, and credit estimate.

    You see a plain-English summary: *"4 draft concepts for exploration, 1 credit each. Then 1 premium final, 8 credits."*
  </Step>

  <Step title="Approve and execute" icon="check-circle">
    Approve the plan. PostGlider dispatches to the right provider:

    | Stage | Quality | Cost |
    |---|---|---|
    | Explore (drafts) | Standard | 1 credit each |
    | Final | Premium | 8 credits |
    | Identity-locked final | Premium (with your headshot) | 8 credits |

    Image generation jobs are async — results appear live in your activity feed as they finish.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Pick your favorite" icon="star">
    If you ran the explore stage, you'll see multiple drafts to choose from. Select one to promote to final, or approve a draft as-is to save credits.
  </Step>
</Steps>
