---
title: Creating a Post
description: |-


  Follow these steps to create your first post in PostGlider:
---
**1. Log in to your dashboard &#x20;**

**Access your PostGlider account.**

**2. Click on "Create Post" &#x20;**

**Locate and click the button to start a new post.**

**3. Write your content &#x20;**

**Enter your caption or content for the post.**

**4. Select social platform &#x20;**

**Choose the platform you want to publish to (e.g. Facebook, Instagram).**

**5. Add media (optional) &#x20;**

**Upload images or videos if needed.**

**6. Preview your post &#x20;**

**Check how your post will appear.**

**7. Publish or Schedule &#x20;**

**Choose to publish immediately or schedule for later.**

**Your post is now ready!**
