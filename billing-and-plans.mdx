## Credits explained

PostGlider uses a credit system instead of charging per feature. Most everyday actions — generating ideas, creating image prompts, reviewing your week — cost nothing. Credits are consumed only by compute-intensive AI operations.

### What uses credits

| Action | Credit cost |
|---|---|
| Generating a batch of ideas | ~1–3 credits |
| AI image generation (per image) | ~5–10 credits |
| Quality scanning (per image) | <1 credit |
| GMB photo import (per batch) | <1 credit |
| Business enrichment (first scan) | ~3 credits |

<Callout kind="info">
  Swiping ideas, editing captions, building week packs, and publishing to social accounts use **no credits**.
</Callout>

### Daily vs monthly limits

Every plan has two limits:

- **Daily limit** — resets at midnight (your local timezone)
- **Monthly limit** — resets on your billing anniversary date

Both limits are visible on your **Dashboard** and **Billing** page. If you hit your daily limit, generation resumes at midnight. If you hit your monthly limit, you'll need to wait for your next billing cycle or upgrade your plan.

## Upgrading your plan

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the Billing page" icon="credit-card">
    Click **Billing** in the sidebar. You'll see your current tier and usage.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Upgrade" icon="arrow-up-circle">
    Click the **Upgrade** button. You'll be taken to the checkout page where you can choose Pro or Pro Plus.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete checkout" icon="check-circle">
    Enter your payment details and confirm. Your new tier activates immediately — no restart needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Annual billing is available and saves roughly 20% compared to month-to-month pricing. Look for the billing cycle toggle on the pricing page.
</Callout>

## Managing your subscription

Click **Manage Subscription** on the Billing page to open the customer portal. From there you can:

- View your billing history and download invoices
- Update your payment method
- Change your billing cycle (monthly ↔ annual)
- Cancel your subscription

Cancellations take effect at the end of your current billing period. You keep full access until then.

## What happens when you downgrade or cancel

If you downgrade from Pro Plus to Pro (or to Free), your profiles, ideas, and vault images are preserved. The only thing that changes is your credit limits and access to features like Pilot Mode and Butler.

<Callout kind="warning">
  If you have more profiles than your new tier allows, you won't lose them — but you won't be able to create new ones until you're back within the limit.
</Callout>
