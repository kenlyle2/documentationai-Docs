## Overview

<Image src="https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1676276375914-cc102df16fa9?q=85&w=1100&h=450&fit=crop" width="1100" height="450" alt="Team planning social media content calendar on a whiteboard with sections for Twitter, TikTok, Instagram, and Facebook." />

PostGlider analyzes your business type, target audience, and platform trends to deliver personalized recommendations. Access everything through a simple dashboard at `https://dashboard.example.com`.

## Key Benefits for Local Businesses

You save hours each week on content planning. PostGlider ensures your posts engage customers at peak times, boosting visibility and interactions. Key advantages include:

- **Consistency**: Daily suggestions keep your feed active.

- **Relevance**: Tailored ideas match your local market.

- **Efficiency**: Copy-paste captions and schedules ready to go.

## Getting Started Overview

Begin using PostGlider in minutes. Follow these steps to generate your first post ideas:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Account" icon="user-plus" title-type="p">
    Sign up at `https://dashboard.example.com/signup` with your email.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure Business" icon="settings" title-type="p">
    Enter your business type, location, and target audience.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Generate Ideas" icon="zap" title-type="p">
    Review daily suggestions in the dashboard.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Schedule & Post" icon="calendar" title-type="p">
    Copy captions and post at suggested times.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<CodeGroup show-lines="true" tabs={[]}>
  ```javascript
  // Fetch daily ideas via API
  const response = await fetch('https://api.example.com/v1/ideas?businessId=YOUR_BUSINESS_ID', {
    headers: { Authorization: `Bearer ${YOUR_API_KEY}` }
  });
  const ideas = await response.json();
  console.log(ideas);
  ```

  ```bash
  # CLI example for quick setup
  curl -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_API_KEY" \
       https://api.example.com/v1/ideas?businessId=YOUR_BUSINESS_ID
  ```
</CodeGroup>
