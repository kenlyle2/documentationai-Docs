## What PostGlider Does

PostGlider turns a small business's existing photos, customer reviews, and service list into a ready-to-publish week of social media content. The whole pipeline — research, writing, image generation, and scheduling — runs in minutes.

You never start from a blank screen. PostGlider researches your business first, then generates content tailored to your industry, location, and real customer reviews.

## Who It's For

PostGlider is built for small business owners who need to show up consistently on social media but don't have time to think about it every day. Tattoo studios, landscapers, restaurants, construction companies, salons — any local business with photos and a Google presence.

## The Intelligence Layer

PostGlider doesn't just generate generic content. Every suggestion is informed by:

- **Your Google Business Profile** — real customer review themes surface as post angles
- **Outscraper enrichment** — business details, peak hours, and services auto-populated from your website
- **Social signals** — best days and formats to post by industry and platform
- **Content balance** — tracks whether you've been over-posting promotions vs. education vs. personal content
- **Weather signals** — rain-prep angles for weather-sensitive industries when forecasts change
