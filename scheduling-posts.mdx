---
title: Scheduling Posts
description: |-

  PostGlider allows you to schedule posts to be published at a later time.
---
**Follow these steps:**

**1. Log in to your dashboard &#x20;**

**2. Click on "Create Post" &#x20;**

**3. Write your content &#x20;**

**4. Select your social media platform &#x20;**

**5. Choose a date and time for publishing &#x20;**

**6. Click on "Schedule" to confirm &#x20;**

**Your post will be automatically published at the selected time.**
