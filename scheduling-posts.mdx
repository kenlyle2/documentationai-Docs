## What Is a Week Pack?

A Week Pack is a 7-slot content calendar for a single week. Each slot holds one approved idea — concept, caption, and final image. Once a pack is assembled and images are finalized, you publish directly to social platforms.

## Pack Lifecycle

| Status | What it means |
|---|---|
| `building` | Ideas are being added; not all 7 slots filled |
| `ready_for_upgrade` | All slots have draft images; you can upgrade to finals |
| `upgrading` | Final image generation in progress |
| `ready_to_export` | All images finalized; ready to schedule |
| `exported` | Published to social platforms |

## Weekly Architect

The Architect assembles a full 7-day calendar from your backlog and vault in seconds. No AI call — it's a pure algorithm, which means it's instant.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Scans your backlog" icon="list">
    Looks at all saved ideas not yet in a pack. Filters out seasonally irrelevant content — an idea referencing Christmas gets skipped in April.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scores vault images for freshness" icon="image">
    Images used in recent packs get deprioritized so the same photo doesn't appear every week.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Checks content balance" icon="scale">
    Ensures the week has a healthy mix across the three content types: **Promote** (offers, services), **Provide** (tips, education), and **Personalize** (team, community, behind-the-scenes).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Applies weather signals" icon="cloud">
    For weather-sensitive industries (lawn care, landscaping, outdoor services), rain or storm forecasts trigger weather-prep ideas to be slotted on the right day.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fills gaps" icon="plus-circle">
    Any slot without a matching backlog idea gets a `gap_generate` marker. The Architect notes which slots need a new idea generated.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You review and assemble" icon="check-square">
    You see the 7-day plan. Click **Assemble Pack** to create the Week Pack and promote all matched ideas to "approved" status.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="info">
  Weekly Architect is available on Pro Plus. It requires at least a few saved ideas in your backlog to work with — use the Content Studio to build that backlog first.
</Callout>

## Butler AI — Automatic Pairing

Butler runs in the background and answers one question: *which vault images belong with which pending ideas?*

It uses keyword and semantic overlap between your image tags and idea concepts. No extra AI call needed — images are already tagged. Butler generates **Golden Pairs** (idea + image) scored by confidence (0.0–1.0).

The best pairs surface in the **Curation Drawer** on the generate page. Each pair shows:
- A confidence score
- A plain-English reasoning string: *"This outdoor tools photo directly matches the 'spring cleanup tips' concept."*

One tap approves a pair — the idea is promoted to "approved" and slotted into your active Week Pack.

<Callout kind="tip">
  If Butler finds 6 or more matching pairs for a full 7-day week, it fires a **"Week Ready"** notification to alert you that a complete content calendar is ready to review.
</Callout>

**Availability:** Pro Plus, unlocks on the same drip schedule as Pilot Mode.

## Actions on a Pack

Once a pack is assembled, you manage it from the `/packs` page:

- **View calendar** — 7-day grid with content category labels and cadence notes per day
- **Draft images** — Generate quick draft images for any idea missing one (1 credit each)
- **Upgrade to final** — Full Pilot Mode render for a single slot (8 credits)
- **Reorder / delete** — Drag-and-drop slots
- **Export** — Publish directly to social platforms via UploadPost
