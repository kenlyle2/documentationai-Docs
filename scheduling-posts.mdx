---
title: Scheduling Posts
description: |-

  PostGlider allows you to schedule posts to be published at a later time.

  Follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your dashboard  
  2. Click on "Create Post"  
  3. Write your content  
  4. Select your social media platform  
  5. Choose a date and time for publishing  
  6. Click on "Schedule" to confirm  

  Your post will be automatically published at the selected time.
