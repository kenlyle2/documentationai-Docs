  Quickstart Guide

  Follow these steps to start using PostGlider:

  1. Create an Account

## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info" collapsed="false">
  Ensure you have:

  - An email address for account registration

  - Active social media accounts on Instagram, X (Twitter), Facebook, TikTok, or YouTube

  - A local business profile ready to customize (business name, niche, target audience)
</Callout>

## Create Your PostGlider Account

Follow these steps to sign up and access your dashboard.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Signup Page" icon="external-link" title-type="p">
    Navigate to [https://postglider.vercel.app/start](https://postglider.vercel.app/start) and click `Try Free` or `Sign Up`.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Your Details" icon="user" title-type="p">
    Provide your email, create a password, and enter your business name. Accept the terms and click `Create Account`.

    <Callout kind="tip" collapsed="false">
      Use a business email for easier account recovery.
    </Callout>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail" title-type="p">
    Check your inbox for a verification email from PostGlider and click the link to activate your account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Connect Social Media Accounts

PostGlider supports major platforms. Use the dashboard's `Connect Accounts` section to link them one by one.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Instagram" icon="instagram">
    Click `Connect Instagram`, log in to your Meta account, select your business profile, and authorize PostGlider.
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="X (Twitter)" icon="twitter">
    Select `Connect X`, sign in with your X credentials, and grant permission for posting and scheduling.
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Facebook" icon="facebook">
    Go to `Connect Facebook`, log into Facebook Business Manager, and link your Page.
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="TikTok" icon="tiktok">
    Choose `Connect TikTok`, authenticate your account, and enable content generation access.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Customize Your Profile

Tailor PostGlider to your business for personalized post ideas.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Set Business Details" icon="settings" title-type="p">
    In the dashboard, go to `Profile > Business Info`. Enter your niche (e.g., "coffee shop"), location, and target audience (e.g., "local families").
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose Posting Preferences" icon="calendar" title-type="p">
    Select preferred posting times, tone (e.g., "friendly and engaging"), and content themes (e.g., "promotions, behind-the-scenes").
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save Changes" icon="check-circle" title-type="p">
    Review and save. PostGlider will generate a sample weekly calendar.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Generate and Schedule Your First Post

Now create your first content idea.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Request Post Idea" icon="zap" title-type="p">
    Click `Generate Daily Post` in the dashboard. PostGlider analyzes your profile and suggests a tailored idea.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review and Edit" icon="edit" title-type="p">
    View the caption, hashtags, and optimal timing. Edit as needed.

    Here's an example generated post:

    ```json
    {
      "platform": "Instagram",
      "caption": "Freshly brewed coffee awaits ☕ What's your go-to order? Drop it in the comments! #LocalCoffee #MorningBrew",
      "hashtags": ["CoffeeShop", "LocalBusiness", "GoodMorning"],
      "optimalTime": "2024-10-15T08:00:00Z",
      "imageSuggestion": "Cozy coffee cup steam rising"
    }
    ```
  </Step>

  <Step title="Schedule or Post Now" icon="schedule" title-type="p">
    Click `Schedule` to add to your calendar or `Post Now` for immediate publishing.
  </Step>
</Steps>
