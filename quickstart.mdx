## Before You Begin

<Callout kind="info">
  You don't need an account to get started. PostGlider generates your first post before you sign up — your session, profile, and first idea are all saved when you claim your account at the end.
</Callout>

All you need:
- Your business website URL (or Google Business Profile name if you don't have a site)
- A photo of your workspace or storefront (optional — PostGlider imports from Google if it finds you)

## Get Your First Post

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to postglider.com and click Get Started" icon="external-link" title-type="p">
    Navigate to the `/start` page. You'll see a single question: **"What should I post today?"**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter your business website URL" icon="globe" title-type="p">
    Type your URL and click **Import My Site**. PostGlider begins researching your business in the background — you don't wait for it to finish before moving on.

    <Callout kind="tip">
      Include the full URL with `https://`. If you don't have a website, enter your business name and city instead.
    </Callout>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm your Google Business Profile" icon="map-pin" title-type="p">
    If PostGlider finds your Google listing, it shows a confirmation card with your business photo. Click **Yes, that's me** to import your GMB photos directly into your Media Vault — pre-tagged and quality-scored.

    If you're not found on Google, you'll be prompted to upload a workspace photo instead.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review your profile" icon="user" title-type="p">
    PostGlider shows a pre-filled profile: business name, location, industry, services, and brand tone — all pulled from your website and Google listing. Confirm or adjust anything in about 90 seconds.
  </Step>

  <Step title="See your first post" icon="sparkles" title-type="p">
    PostGlider generates a complete post: an AI-written concept and caption, plus a photorealistic image. You can regenerate as many times as you like before saving.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Claim your account" icon="check-circle" title-type="p">
    Set an email and password. Your session, profile, and first post are all preserved. You land on the Dashboard, ready to generate more.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What Happens Next

After your account is created, you're in the **Dashboard**. From here:

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Generate more content" icon="wand-sparkles" href="/creating-a-post">
    Use the Content Studio to pick themes and generate a full batch of post ideas.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Build your first Week Pack" icon="calendar" href="/scheduling-posts">
    Use the Weekly Architect to assemble a full 7-day calendar in seconds.
  </Card>
</Columns>

<Callout kind="tip">
  **Pilot Mode** unlocks after 7 days on a Pro plan. It gives you full control over image generation — provider, quality tier, identity-locked renders, and style overrides. Worth the wait.
</Callout>
