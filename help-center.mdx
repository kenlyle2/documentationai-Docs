## Quick Answers

<Expandable title="How do credits work?">
  Credits are consumed each time you generate post ideas or perform an AI action. Your balance is shown on the Dashboard and resets monthly on your billing date. See [Account Setup](/account-setup#credits) for a full cost breakdown by action.
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="Can I switch plans?">
  Yes. Go to **My Account → Billing** and click **Manage Subscription**. Upgrades take effect immediately; downgrades apply at the next billing cycle.
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="What happens if I run out of credits?">
  Generation actions are blocked until your balance resets or you upgrade. Your existing content, scheduled posts, and vault images are unaffected. Credits reset on your monthly billing date.
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="How do I connect my Google Business Profile?">
  PostGlider searches for your GMB listing automatically during onboarding. If it wasn't found then, open the Media Vault and use the **Import from Google** button in the vault header.
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="My images aren't appearing in my WordPress gallery search.">
  New images sync to WordPress automatically after AI tagging. If images are missing, ask your admin to run a tag backlog sync. You can also verify your WordPress gallery token in **Settings → WordPress Integration**.
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="How do I cancel?">
  Go to **My Account → Billing** and click **Manage Subscription**. Cancel directly from the billing page. Access continues until the end of the current billing period.
</Expandable>
