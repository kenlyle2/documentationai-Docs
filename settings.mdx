## Image defaults

The **Settings** page (`/settings`) controls the visual defaults applied to every AI-generated image. These settings are account-wide and apply to all your business profiles.

### Title font size

The size (in points) of the headline text overlaid on AI-generated images. Range: 8–96.

The default works well for most images. Increase it if your titles tend to be short and you want them to fill more of the frame. Decrease it if you're using longer titles that run over multiple lines at the default size.

### Title color

The color of the headline text. Click the color swatch to open a color picker. Enter a hex value directly if you know your brand color.

<Callout kind="tip">
  Choose a color with strong contrast against typical photo backgrounds — white or light cream works on most outdoor and lifestyle photos; dark charcoal or black works better on light studio backgrounds.
</Callout>

### Caption font size

The size of the supporting text (caption, call to action) on AI-generated images. Range: 8–72.

Caption text is typically smaller than the title. A good starting ratio is title at 36–48pt, caption at 18–24pt.

### Caption color

The color of the caption text. Often matches or complements the title color.

## Saving changes

Click **Save** after changing any setting. Changes take effect on the next image you generate — existing generated images are not retroactively updated.

<Callout kind="info">
  These settings only affect AI-generated images created through the Weekly Architect and image upgrade pipeline. They don't affect photos you upload manually to the Media Vault.
</Callout>
