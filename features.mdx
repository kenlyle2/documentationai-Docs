## Auto-Tagging

Every image is tagged automatically by **Gemini Vision** (OpenAI as fallback) with two layers of tags:

| Layer | Examples |
|---|---|
| **Visual tags** | `outdoor space`, `autumn leaves`, `person smiling`, `red dragon`, `water feature` |
| **Service tags** | `lawn care`, `before/after transformation`, `team culture`, `client spotlight` |

Both layers merge into a single searchable tag set. If you've defined business-specific terms in your profile, the tagger uses those over generic equivalents.

Tags are written automatically when you upload an image or import from GMB. They can be manually edited or regenerated with **AI Re-tag** from the image detail dialog.

## Smart Search

Search your vault with natural language. Type "rainy day photos" or "large back pieces" or "team holding tools" and PostGlider returns the most relevant images.

Smart Search uses semantic matching against tags and descriptions — not just keyword matching. Toggle it on with the **Brain** icon in the search bar.

<Callout kind="tip">
  Smart Search is especially powerful for businesses with 50+ images. Use it to surface the right photo for a specific campaign without scrolling through everything.
</Callout>

## Quality Scoring

The AI scores each image on marketing quality: composition, lighting, subject clarity, and brand fit. Scores run from 1–10.

Run a bulk quality scan from the **Tools** page (`/tools`):
- See how many images are unscored
- Scan all unscored images in one click
- Set a target vault size — the Vault Cleaner identifies the lowest-scoring images to remove

<Callout kind="info">
  Quality scores affect search result ranking — higher-scoring images surface first in WordPress gallery search results.
</Callout>

## Archiving

Archive any image from the hover overlay or the image detail dialog. Archived images:
- Move to the **Archived** filter tab — they're not deleted
- Are excluded from the active grid and Vault Cleaner operations
- Can be unarchived at any time

Use archiving for seasonal content or images you want to keep but don't need in rotation.

## GMB Import

Connect your Google Business Profile (one-time OAuth from the vault page) to import your GMB photos directly. Imported photos arrive:
- Pre-tagged with their GMB labels
- Quality-scored automatically
- Organized in the **My Uploads** filter

You can re-import at any time to pick up new GMB photos you've added.

## Profile Media Slots

If you have a business profile set up, the vault shows a **Profile Media** tab. Each profile has named image slots — **Headshot 1**, **Logo**, **Workspace**, **Team Photo** — that the image generation pipeline uses when building content specific to your brand.

Assign vault images to these slots from the profile tab to ensure PostGlider always uses the right photos for identity-locked Pilot Mode renders.

## Competitor Intelligence

From **Settings**, you maintain a short list of local competitors. PostGlider's Competitor Finder discovers competitors automatically from your NAICS/SIC industry codes and enriches them with GMB data and social signals.

This data feeds into Genie's suggestion engine — you'll see differentiation-focused angles ("while others charge by the hour, you charge by the project") when relevant.

## Social Intelligence Signals

Available to all authenticated users, these signals inform timing and format decisions:

| Signal | What it tells you |
|---|---|
| **Day Signal** | Best days to post by your industry and platform |
| **Format Signal** | Whether video, carousel, or single image performs best for your niche |
| **Hashtag Signal** | Outperforming, underperforming, and fresh hashtag recommendations by niche |
