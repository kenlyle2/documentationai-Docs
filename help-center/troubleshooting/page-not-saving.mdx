description: Troubleshoot page saving issues in PostGlider.
---

## Issue

- Edits to a doc page do not persist
- No confirmation after clicking 'Save'

## Steps to Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Connection">
    ````bash
    # Ensure you are online
    ping app.postglider.com
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refresh Session">
    Log out, then log in again to refresh permissions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact Support">
    If the problem remains, email support@postglider.com with details.
  </Step>
</Steps>
