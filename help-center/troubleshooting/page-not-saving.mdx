## Settings Not Saving

**Symptom:** You click Save in Settings but changes don't persist after reload.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check your internet connection" icon="wifi">
    Settings saves via a live API call. A dropped connection will silently fail. Refresh the page and try again.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Look for an error toast" icon="alert-triangle">
    A red toast should appear if the save failed. If you see "Session expired," sign out and back in, then retry.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Try a hard refresh" icon="refresh-cw">
    Press `Ctrl+Shift+R` (Windows/Linux) or `Cmd+Shift+R` (Mac) to clear the page cache, then try saving again.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact support" icon="mail">
    If the issue persists, email support@postglider.com with your account email and a description of which setting you're trying to save.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Images Not Loading in the Vault

**Symptom:** Image cards appear blank or broken in the Media Vault.

<Callout kind="info">
  Images are served from PostGlider's image CDN. Occasional propagation delays (under 30 seconds) can cause new uploads to appear blank briefly.
</Callout>

- Wait 30 seconds and refresh the page — most cases resolve on their own.
- If only older images are broken, the CDN URL may have changed. Contact support.
- If no images appear at all, check that you're on the correct business profile tab at the top of the Vault.

## Generation Not Starting

**Symptom:** You click Generate and nothing happens, or the button is grayed out.

- **Out of credits:** Your credit balance is shown on the Dashboard. If it's 0, generation is blocked until your billing cycle resets.
- **Turnstile challenge:** PostGlider uses Cloudflare Turnstile to prevent abuse. If it can't verify you automatically, a brief challenge appears. Complete it and try again.
- **No business profile selected:** Make sure you have at least one business profile set up under **My Vibe → Business Profiles**.

## WordPress Gallery Search Not Working

**Symptom:** Searching on your WordPress site returns no results, or images are missing from search.

- Verify your gallery token is still valid: **Settings → WordPress Integration** — look for the token status indicator.
- New images need to be AI-tagged before they sync to WordPress. Untagged images won't appear in search.
- Ask your admin to run a tag backlog sync to push all existing images to WordPress.
- If the SearchIQ index hasn't been crawled recently, trigger a fresh crawl from the SearchIQ plugin settings in WordPress admin.

## Magic Link / Email Not Arriving

**Symptom:** You requested a password reset or magic link but haven't received the email.

1. Check your spam and junk folders.
2. Wait up to 2 minutes — transactional email can be delayed.
3. Make sure you're checking the correct email address associated with your PostGlider account.
4. If still nothing, contact support@postglider.com. We can confirm your email address and resend manually.
