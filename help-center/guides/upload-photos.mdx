## Before you begin

Photos you upload feed directly into your content pipeline. The AI tagger reads each image and adds descriptive tags — so when PostGlider generates content, it can pair the right image with the right post.

The best vault photos are:
- In focus and well-lit
- Showing your actual work, space, team, or products
- At least 800×800px (larger is fine)
- JPEG or PNG format

## Option 1: Upload from your phone or computer

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Media Vault" icon="image">
    Tap **Media Vault** in the sidebar. You'll see your existing images and an upload area at the top.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap the upload zone" icon="upload">
    On mobile, tap the upload area. On desktop, drag files directly onto it or click to open the file picker.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for tagging to finish" icon="tag">
    PostGlider automatically tags each uploaded image with visual and service-specific labels. This takes a few seconds per image. You'll see a progress indicator in the image card.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a description (optional)" icon="pen-line">
    Click any image to open the detail dialog. Add a description to give the AI more context about what's in the photo — this improves search results and content pairing.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Option 2: Import from Google Business Profile

If your Google Business Profile is connected, you can pull all your GMB photos into the vault at once.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Media Vault" icon="image">
    Tap **Media Vault** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the GMB Import option" icon="map-pin">
    Look for the **Import from Google** button near the top of the vault. If you haven't connected your GMB account yet, you'll be prompted to connect it first.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select photos to import" icon="check-square">
    A grid of your GMB photos appears. Select the ones you want to bring into PostGlider, then click **Import selected**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Photos are tagged automatically" icon="tag">
    Imported photos are tagged with your GMB categories and service context — no manual tagging needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
  GMB photos are often your best vault images. They're real shots of your actual business that potential customers have already seen and responded to.
</Callout>

## Option 3: Assign photos to profile slots

Once photos are in your vault, you can assign them to specific **profile slots** — named positions like Headshot, Workspace Interior, or Team Group Photo.

Profile slots feed directly into Week Pack image generation. When PostGlider builds a week of content, it pulls from the appropriate slot for each post type.

To assign a photo:
1. Go to the vault and click the **profile tab** (your business name, next to "All Images")
2. You'll see the profile slot grid — slots you've filled and slots that are empty
3. Click any empty slot (or an existing one to change it)
4. Pick the image from your vault you want in that slot

## Saving tags and descriptions

After uploading or editing an image, click **Save Photo and Tags** in the image detail dialog to store your changes.

<Callout kind="info">
  Tags are saved automatically after AI tagging runs. You only need to click **Save Photo and Tags** if you manually edit tags or add a description.
</Callout>
