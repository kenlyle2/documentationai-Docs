## Quick Start

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Dashboard">
    Visit `https://app.postglider.com/dashboard` and log in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'New Project'">
    Press the <b>New Project</b> button at the top right.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill Project Details">
    Enter a name and brief description, then click <b>Create</b>.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Documenting">
    Begin adding and organizing pages within your new project.
  </Step>
</Steps>
