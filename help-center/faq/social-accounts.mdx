## Which platforms does PostGlider support?

PostGlider currently connects to Google Business Profile, Facebook Pages, and LinkedIn (personal profile or company page).

## Do I have to connect a social account?

No. You can use PostGlider to generate and download content without connecting any social accounts. Connecting platforms enables direct publishing — PostGlider sends approved ideas there automatically when Butler is active.

## My Google Business Profile wasn't found during onboarding. What now?

PostGlider searches by your business name and location. If it didn't find your listing, try:

1. Going to **Social Accounts** and connecting GMB manually using the OAuth flow
2. Making sure your Google Business Profile is verified and public
3. Checking that your business name in PostGlider matches exactly what's on your Google listing

If you still can't connect, you can complete onboarding without GMB — your vault won't have imported photos, but you can upload your own.

## Can I connect more than one Facebook Page?

Each PostGlider account connects to one Facebook Page at a time. If you manage multiple pages, you'll need separate PostGlider accounts (available on Enterprise plans).

## My connection says "expired." How do I fix it?

OAuth tokens expire after a period of inactivity. Click **Reconnect** on the platform card in Social Accounts. You'll go through the sign-in flow again — it takes about 30 seconds. Your settings and publish history are preserved.

## PostGlider published something I didn't want it to publish.

If Butler is active and a platform is set as your default, PostGlider will publish approved ideas automatically. To prevent this:

- Remove the **Default** setting from the platform card
- Or pause Butler from the Content Studio

You can delete any post directly from the platform (Google, Facebook, LinkedIn) if it went out in error.

## How do I disconnect a platform?

Go to **Social Accounts** and click **Disconnect** on the platform card. The connection is removed immediately. Your content on the platform is not affected.
