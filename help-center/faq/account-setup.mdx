title: How do I create an account?
description: Guide to account creation in PostGlider.
---

## Quick Steps

1. Go to `https://app.postglider.com/signup`  
2. Enter your email and choose a password
3. Confirm your email via the verification link
4. Log in at `https://app.postglider.com/login`

## Tip

<Callout kind="info">
If you don't see the verification email, check your spam or junk folder.
</Callout>
