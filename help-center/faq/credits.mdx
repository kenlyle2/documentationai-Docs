## What are credits?

Credits are the currency PostGlider uses to measure AI compute. Most actions in the app — browsing ideas, editing captions, reviewing week packs — are free. Credits are only consumed when PostGlider runs AI operations on your behalf.

## What uses credits?

| Action | Approximate cost |
|---|---|
| Generating a batch of post ideas | 1–3 credits |
| AI image generation | 5–10 credits per image |
| Quality scanning | Less than 1 credit per image |
| Business enrichment (first scan) | ~3 credits |
| GMB photo import | Less than 1 credit |

## I ran out of credits. What do I do?

**If you hit your daily limit:** Wait until midnight. Daily credits reset every 24 hours at midnight in your local timezone.

**If you hit your monthly limit:** Your credits will reset on your next billing anniversary date. If you need more immediately, you can upgrade to a higher plan from the Billing page.

## Where can I see how many credits I have left?

Your credit usage appears in two places:
- **Dashboard** — stat cards showing today's and this month's usage
- **Billing page** — a full breakdown of your limits and current usage

## Do unused credits roll over?

No. Credits reset on their respective daily and monthly schedules regardless of how many you used. Unused daily credits do not carry over to the next day.

## Can I buy more credits without upgrading my plan?

Not at this time. Credit limits are set per tier. Upgrading to the next tier is the way to increase your limits.
