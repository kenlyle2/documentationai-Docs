## Overview

The **Tools** page (`/tools`) gives you two maintenance utilities for your Media Vault: quality scanning and vault cleanup. Neither is required — your vault works fine without them — but they become valuable once you have 50+ images.

## Quality scanning

PostGlider can rate every image in your vault on a 1–10 scale for marketing quality. The score reflects:

- **Clarity** — is the subject sharp and in focus?
- **Lighting** — is the scene well-lit with no harsh shadows?
- **Subject prominence** — does the main subject fill the frame appropriately?
- **Marketing fit** — does the image look like it belongs in a professional social post?

### Running a quality scan

The **Quality Scanning** section shows how many of your images have been scored and how many haven't. Click **Scan unscanned** to score every unscanned image in one batch.

The scan runs in the background. You'll see a progress counter update as each image is processed.

### Score distribution

After scanning, you'll see a breakdown of your vault by score tier:

| Tier | Score range | What it means |
|---|---|---|
| **High** | 8–10 | Strong marketing images — surface these first |
| **Mid** | 5–7 | Usable but not standout — fine for general use |
| **Low** | 1–4 | Weak technical quality — consider replacing or archiving |

<Callout kind="tip">
  Quality scores affect search result ordering in the Media Vault and in WordPress gallery search. Higher-scored images appear at the top of relevant results.
</Callout>

## Vault cleaner

The Vault Cleaner identifies images that are taking up storage without contributing much value, and recommends whether to archive or delete them.

### How it works

The cleaner looks at two factors:

1. **Usage** — how many times the image has been included in generated ideas
2. **Quality score** — how the image scores on marketing quality

It produces two candidate lists:

| List | Candidates | Recommended action |
|---|---|---|
| **Archive candidates** | Images used 2+ times but with lower quality scores | Archive — you've used them, but there are better alternatives |
| **Delete candidates** | Images with zero usage and older than 30 days | Delete — you've never used them, they're just taking up storage |

### Running the cleaner

Review both candidate lists. Each row shows a thumbnail, quality score, use count, and upload date. 

Click **Execute recommended cleanup** to apply all recommendations at once, or act on individual images using the Archive or Delete buttons on each row.

<Callout kind="warning">
  Archived images are hidden from the active vault but are recoverable. Deleted images are permanently removed. Review the delete list carefully before confirming.
</Callout>

### Target storage size

You can set a target vault size (in MB) in the cleaner. PostGlider will calculate how many images need to be removed to hit that target and prioritize the lowest-scoring, least-used images in the recommendations.
