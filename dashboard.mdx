## Your dashboard at a glance

The dashboard (`/dashboard`) is a read-only overview of your content activity. Nothing on this page changes your content — it's a mirror of what's already happened so you can spot patterns and stay on track.

## Streak

The streak counter at the top shows how many consecutive days you've generated at least one idea. It resets to zero if a full calendar day passes without any generation activity.

The streak is primarily motivational — it rewards daily engagement, which correlates with businesses that build a consistent social presence over time.

## Activity chart

A 30-day rolling chart of your daily idea generation. Each bar represents one day; the height reflects how many ideas were generated. Hover over any bar to see the exact count.

Use the activity chart to:
- Identify your most active days
- Spot gaps where you missed content
- See whether your pace is picking up or slowing down

## Stat cards

| Card | What it measures |
|---|---|
| **Total ideas** | All ideas ever generated, regardless of status |
| **AI images** | Images generated by the AI image pipeline |
| **Packs exported** | Week Packs that have been exported for scheduling |
| **Monthly usage** | Credits used this calendar month vs. your monthly limit |
| **Daily usage** | Credits used today vs. your daily limit |

<Callout kind="info">
  Credits are consumed when you generate ideas, run AI image jobs, or use quality scanning. See the [Billing & Plans](/billing-and-plans) page for a full breakdown of what uses credits.
</Callout>

## Idea stage breakdown

A visual breakdown of where your ideas currently stand:

| Stage | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **In Review** | Generated but not yet saved or skipped |
| **Approved** | Saved ideas ready to use |
| **Draft** | Ideas in Week Packs with a draft image |
| **Explore** | Ideas paired with an AI image candidate |
| **Final** | Ideas with a confirmed final image |
| **Exported** | Ideas that have been scheduled or published |

The breakdown updates in real time as you work through ideas in the Content Studio.

## Best days chart

PostGlider analyzes engagement benchmarks for your industry and recommends the best days to publish. The chart shows expected relative reach by day of week.

<Callout kind="tip">
  The best days recommendation is based on industry averages, not your specific account analytics. For more precise timing, check your platform's native insights after you've been publishing for 30+ days.
</Callout>

## Enrichment status

If PostGlider has detected that your business data needs updating — a new GMB listing found, reviews refreshed, or a website re-scan available — a banner appears at the top of the dashboard. Click it to review what's new and confirm or dismiss the update.
