## How to Upgrade

Follow these steps to ensure you access the latest features seamlessly.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Dashboard" icon="monitor">
    Log in to your PostGlider account at `https://app.postglider.com`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Changes" icon="book-open">
    Visit this changelog and note any breaking changes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refresh Session" icon="refresh-cw">
    Clear your browser cache or log out and back in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test New Features" icon="play">
    Generate a new post idea to experience improvements.
  </Step>
</Steps>

  For v1.2.0+, enable beta features in your settings to try upcoming enhancements early.
